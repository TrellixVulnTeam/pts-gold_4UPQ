PTS K9 Training Methods



At PTSk9 we use the most up-to-date in methods and equipment for training the many dogs that are brought to us to learn, we use a balance of reward based training using favorite foods, games or toys, and praises as well as affection as motivation for the dogs to learn.

We also use corrections for dogs but only after a dog has fully comprehended and understood what action it is that is required from the dog when it is commanded, thereafter if the dog is easily distracted or not complient the dog is corrected through the leash with simple techniques which are neither harmful or painful to the dog. This creates a balance of both positive and negative reenforcement to keep the dog reliable and obedient in both private and public settings.
